Help

To make an account:

  • click on login
  • click create account
  • fill out the information
  • click register

To buy a book:

  • find the book under Books
  • click on the book
  • add to the shopping cart
  • go to the cart
  • click checkout

To update account information:

  • click on profile
  • click update info
  • fill in the information
  • click update
